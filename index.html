Our Identity

A Legacy of
Excellence

MSU-MCEST is a premier public institution in Northern Mindanao. Formerly known as MSU-MSAT, it was officially renamed to reflect its expanded structural vision in education, science, and technology.

Recognized by CHED Region 10 with the prestigious ASEAN Quality Award, the institution serves as a lighthouse for academic rigor and student development.
